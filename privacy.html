Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10 2026

Protecting your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in relation to your use of the ScenInk mobile application.

1. Data Collection

The ScenInk app is designed with a privacy-first approach.

No Personal Data: We do not collect, store, or share any personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

No Tracking: We do not track your location, your contacts, or your activity within the app.

No Third-Party Services: We do not use third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising networks that collect user data.

2. Local Storage

Any data or settings created within the app are stored exclusively on your device. This data is never transmitted to our servers or to third parties.

3. Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect any personal data, the app is safe for users of all ages, including children.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
