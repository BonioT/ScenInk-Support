About ScenInk

ScenInk is designed for comic artists, illustrators, and visual storytellers. It helps simplify complex drawing challenges by combining scene construction, posing, camera exploration, and sketching in one creative workflow.

Scene composition

Build references that help you organize framing, depth, and visual balance.

Character posing

Arrange poses more clearly before committing to your final drawing.

Perspective & anatomy

Use scene and pose references to support difficult angles and body structure.

Storytelling through camera

Explore points of view and camera angles to improve visual narration.

What is ScenInk?

ScenInk is a creative support tool for artists who want to build scenes, pose characters, explore camera angles, and sketch over references.

Who is ScenInk for?

ScenInk is made for comic artists, illustrators, students, and visual storytellers who need help with composition, anatomy, and perspective.
