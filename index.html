About ScenInk

ScenInk is designed to simplify comic creation and visual storytelling by helping artists handle scene composition, character posing, perspective, anatomy, and storytelling through camera angles.

Scene composition

Build references that help organize framing, depth, and visual balance.

Character posing

Adjust poses before committing to the final drawing.

Perspective & anatomy

Support difficult angles, body structure, and complex hand references.

Storytelling through camera

Explore camera angles and points of view to improve visual narration.

FAQ

What is ScenInk?

ScenInk is a creative support tool for artists who want to build scenes, pose characters, explore camera angles, and sketch over references.

Who is ScenInk for?

ScenInk is made for comic artists, illustrators, students, and visual storytellers who need help with composition, anatomy, and perspective.

How can I report a bug?

You can report bugs by emailing us at tonybonny2001@gmail.com with a short description of the issue and, if possible, screenshots.

Is ScenInk available on iPhone and iPad?

ScenInk is currently available only for iPad.

Can I use ScenInk for comic planning and sketching?

Yes. ScenInk is designed to support scene planning, pose exploration, and drawing workflows for comics and visual storytelling.
